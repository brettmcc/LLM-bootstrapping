## My submit-first order

If I were deciding where to spend submission time first, I would prioritize them in this order:

1. APPAM
2. 2026 AI in Social Science Conference (Chicago BFI)
3. 9th CEPR-Banca d'Italia Workshop on Labour Market Policies and Institutions
4. LLMSS 2026
5. LACEA-LAMES 2026
6. ECONDAT 2026
7. BREAD-CEPR Development Economics Annual Symposium 2026
8. SITE 2026 Applied Artificial Intelligence in Macro-Finance

## Short rationale

APPAM is the strongest "realistic and useful" target because it has the best mix of fit and acceptance odds for a preliminary paper on immigration, labor, and empirical methods. The Chicago BFI AI in Social Science conference and LLMSS are the strongest method-facing targets. CEPR-Banca d'Italia is the strongest labor-econ workshop, but it is materially more selective. LACEA-LAMES is the best broad economics backstop. ECONDAT, BREAD-CEPR, and Stanford SITE are real options, but they are clearly more pitch-dependent or more selective than the first five.
